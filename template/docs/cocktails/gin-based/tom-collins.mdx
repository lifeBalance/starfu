# Tom Collins

A gin sour that went to finishing school and came back with bubbles.

## Ingredients

- 2 oz gin
- 1 oz fresh lemon juice
- 1/2 oz simple syrup
- Club soda
- Lemon wheel and cherry for garnish

## Instructions

1. Add gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup to a shaker with ice.

2. Shake until cold, about 15 seconds.

3. Strain into a Collins glass (tall glass) filled with fresh ice.

4. Top with club soda.

5. Garnish with a lemon wheel and cherry.

6. Add a straw if you're civilized.
